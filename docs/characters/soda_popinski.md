## 🥊 Overview

**Soda Popinski** is the iconic Russian southpaw boxer from the *Punch-Out!!* series. Originally introduced as **Vodka Drunkenski** in the 1984 arcade game *Super Punch-Out!!*, his name was changed for the NES release to avoid alcohol references while maintaining his relentless, soda-fueled personality.

Standing at 6'6", Popinski is notorious for:
- Constant soda drinking between rounds (which actively restores his health in-game).
- His distinctive, booming laugh.
- Devastating, brutal uppercuts.
- Being one of the most rule-breaking fighters in the circuit (bringing soda into the ring, drinking during rounds, hiding bottles in his trunks, and doping with laboratory elixirs).

## ⚡ Game Mechanics & Fighting Style

### 1. One Hit Knockdown Condition (NES *Punch-Out!!*)
Soda Popinski can be downed instantly with a single Star Punch under specific timing:
1. Intercept his uppercut with a gut punch (holding `Down` on the directional pad to lock him into a crouching position).
2. Immediately follow up with a **Star Punch**.
3. He goes down in a single blow.

### 2. Title Defense Version (Wii *Punch-Out!!*)
In the Wii version's Title Defense mode, scientists developed a custom **soda elixir** for Popinski:
- **Stat Boosts:** Every drink makes him progressively faster and stronger.
- **Rage/Redness:** Turns visibly redder as the fight progresses, obscuring his attack tells and telegraphs.
- **Vulnerability Trade-off:** The more of his attacks you dodge, the more vulnerable he becomes to counter-attacks.
